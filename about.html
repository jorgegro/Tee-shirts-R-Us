About us

A family business, started with humble beginnings. Our parents died after a terrible accident and the only thing we could do was survive. After so many years of struggle we found our calling. We were creative in many ways. We decided in designing. At the beginning it was just a simple shop. Spray painting logos we design and selling t-shirts in the streets of Dallas. Now we have come long but family never dies and we can safely say to our decesed parents that "we made it!".
